Symbols of Christmas

Christmas Tree

The Christmas tree symbolizes life and renewal. It is often decorated with lights and ornaments.

Nativity Scene

The nativity scene represents the birth of Jesus Christ, highlighting the importance of family and faith.

Santa Claus

Santa Claus symbolizes giving and joy, bringing gifts to children around the world.

Christmas Stockings

Hanging stockings by the fireplace is a tradition where gifts are placed for family members.

Star

The star represents the Star of Bethlehem, guiding the Wise Men to the birthplace of Jesus.
